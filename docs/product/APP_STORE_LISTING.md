## Full Description (Store)
EZRASCORES is a fast, family-friendly football companion built for matchday energy and daily play.

Track live scores, fixtures, and league tables for the English Premier League and Championship. Pick your favourite team, follow their match centre, and challenge family or friends in mini-leagues with score predictions and weekly competition.

Whether you are 7 or 70, EZRASCORES keeps football simple, visual, and fun.

### Why fans use EZRASCORES
- Live match coverage with clear match states: live, upcoming, final score
- Favourite team pin with fast access to match centre and recent form
- Daily quests and football mini-games that reward consistency
- Family mini-leagues with score prediction points and standings
- Club quiz and pop quiz features for younger fans and football experts
- Dream Team builder with exportable visuals
- Smooth, responsive UX across phone, tablet and desktop

### Core Features
- Fixtures and Results
  - Browse by date and league
  - Quick status labels and clean score display
  - Highlights support where available

- League Tables
  - Premier League + Championship tables
  - Server-backed refresh for reliability and speed
  - Clear promotion/relegation visual cues

- Favourite Team Experience
  - Team branding and pinned match view
  - Last 5 form indicators
  - Next fixture context and matchday messaging

- Play & Compete
  - Score predictions with league scoring
  - Daily quests and streak mechanics
  - Family mini-league join/share code flows

- Account (Optional)
  - Cloud save for leagues, progress and picks
  - Recovery email + PIN reset flow

### Built for families, built for football
EZRASCORES is designed to make football more interactive every day, not just during kick-off.
